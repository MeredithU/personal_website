My Story is Pretty Badass



I love seeing my ideas come to life! It's so empowering to know that I can wake up each morning with an idea and then spend the day making it a reality. I am so thankful for each of my past experiences that have brought me to today. After getting my math and physics degree, I worked as a project director in the mobile and video game industries.


One day while I was speaking to a group of 300 high school girls about the benefits of a STEM career, I made the bold statement that I was going to make a career change and become a developer. I left my PM job and went for it!


After arming myself with HTML5, CSS3, Ruby on Rails and Objective-C, I'm ready to dominate. To give back to the community and to inspire women and youth in technology, I am a volunteer web developer for the Ladies Learning Code and TEDxYouth in Toronto organizations.
